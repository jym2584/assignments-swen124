NETWORKING ERRORS

____ - Your server is trying to start listening for connections on some port, but the port is already in use by some other process.

____ - Your client is trying to connect to a port on the server but the server is not listening for connections on that port.

____ - You attempted to read from or write to a socket, but the connection has already been closed.

____ - You are trying to establish a connection to an hostname or address that does not exist on the network.

____ - Any other kind of general error.
